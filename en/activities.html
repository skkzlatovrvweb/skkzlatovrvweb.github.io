100 YEARS OF ZLATOVRV

On November 16th, a ceremonial event was held to celebrate the 100th anniversary of Zlatovrv Club. The climbing club, speleological club, and orientation club presented their contributions and significant achievements in sports. During the celebration, plaques for merits and gratitude for contributions, cooperation, and emerging talents in sports were awarded. Within our club, Prof. Hristo Belakaposki was awarded a plaque for his outstanding contribution to climbing, while club members Mihail Jankoski and Cvete Jandreoski received certificates of appreciation. The event concluded with a planned hiking march to Zlatovrv Peak. We thank all participants for their support and for nurturing a love and passion for sports and the mountains!

PRILEP BOULDER WEEKEND 2024

From September 27th to 29th, the Prilep Boulder Weekend took place under the motto: WOMEN POWER! This event focused on women to promote equal participation in sports, raise awareness, encourage collaboration, and highlight the benefits of sports. Our special guest was the world-renowned Mina Markovic, who selflessly shared her experience and love for climbing with all of us. Once again, SKK Zlatovrv was the organizer of an unforgettable event. We extend our gratitude to TAKT (Together Advancing Common Trust), the official sponsors of the event.

MEMORIAL ALPINIST GATHERING

The "Zoran Majstorski" Memorial Alpinist Gathering is held at Pilav Tepe - Radovish, honoring the Macedonian alpinist and our dear friend. A team from SKK Zlatovrv participated and paid tribute by climbing several new alpine routes. Maria and Mihail, relatively new members, had their first experience climbing in rope teams and successfully climbed alpine routes graded 5a and 5b.
